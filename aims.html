Aims & Objective

This conference will serve as an effective platform for cluster of academic experts and students to exchange the ideas and bridge the gap between applied science, engineering and technology. It is an important milestone in the roadmap towards establishing the connections between the industry institute and the students.
The conference will discuss the impact of transition to Fourth Industrial Revolution on Ecosystem, Infrastructure, Efficiency and Competitiveness of industrial operation in general. We will investigate the macro needs to adapt smart operation in terms of changing connectivity, education and business practices. Also, we will explore the impact of Augmented Reality, Robotics, IOT and Artificial intelligence on value chain support and optimization.
In this conference, we will explore more involvements of innovative technologies, it will enhance maintenance management and real time performance. Also, it will optimize asset utilization and integrate lifecycle. Artificial intelligence will provide decision support system and give better predictive analysis and 3D printing will emerge as new source of spare parts that manufactures need to explore. This conference will identify solutions to improve Logistics Industries efficiency. During this conference we will analyze the smart methods of monitoring and operating engineering activities, examines how Industry 4.0 will improve its resource planning (ERP), estimation, market place and analysis.
The conference will also explore the engineering practices and methods to enhance management of design, scheduling, procurement, quality, control, documentation and safety by utilizing industry 4.0. The conference will share upstream and downstream experiences of applying Industry 4.0 on metal industries and will discuss impacts of automation, robotics and smart sensing on improving production and accuracy. Also, discuss on how digital system is enabling workers to communicate, share experience and develop skills. During this event, there will be discussions on how to increase control, safety, security by integrating system smartly and also investigate how artificial intelligence and data analysis will improve decisions of outsourcing, operation efficiency.
